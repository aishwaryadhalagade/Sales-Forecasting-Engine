## Sales Forecasting Engine

## Project Overview

The Sales Forecasting Engine is a predictive analytics solution designed to forecast future sales demand, revenue trends, and inventory requirements. By leveraging historical sales data, seasonality patterns, promotional activities, and business drivers, the solution enables organizations to improve planning accuracy, optimize inventory levels, and support data-driven decision-making.

The platform combines statistical forecasting methods and machine learning models to generate reliable short-term and long-term sales projections across products, regions, and business units.

---

## Business Problem

Accurate sales forecasting is critical for inventory planning, revenue management, procurement, and operational efficiency. Traditional forecasting approaches often rely on manual estimates and historical trends, making them vulnerable to seasonality changes, demand fluctuations, and market dynamics.

Common business challenges include:

- Inventory shortages and stock-outs
- Excess inventory carrying costs
- Inaccurate revenue projections
- Inefficient demand planning
- Delayed operational decision-making

Organizations require a scalable forecasting framework capable of predicting future demand while adapting to changing business conditions.

---

## Solution

Developed an end-to-end forecasting solution utilizing historical sales transactions, seasonal trends, promotional events, and external business factors to predict future sales performance.

Performed data preprocessing, feature engineering, trend analysis, seasonality decomposition, and model optimization. Built and evaluated multiple forecasting models including ARIMA, Prophet, XGBoost, and LSTM to compare performance across different forecasting horizons.

Implemented automated forecasting pipelines and interactive dashboards to support business planning, inventory management, and executive decision-making.

## Results & Evaluation

### Model Performance

- Achieved forecast accuracy of approximately 88% across key product categories and business segments.
- Reduced forecasting error (MAPE) by nearly 20% compared to baseline forecasting methods.
- Improved demand prediction reliability through feature engineering and model optimization.
- Evaluated forecasting performance using MAPE, RMSE, MAE, and Forecast Accuracy metrics.

- Improved inventory planning accuracy by approximately 15–16%.
- Reduced stock-out risk through proactive demand forecasting.
- Enabled more accurate revenue and sales planning.
- Improved alignment between sales, operations, and inventory teams.
- Reduced manual forecasting effort by nearly 50%.
